# NeoGrid AI Engine

## Purpose

NeoGrid AI turns energy telemetry, forecasts, battery state, grid data, and carbon analytics into operator-approved recommendations.

## Forecast Types

- Solar production forecast
- Load demand forecast
- Battery state forecast
- Grid dependency forecast
- Carbon impact forecast
- Maintenance risk forecast

## Recommendation Types

- Charge battery before forecasted low production
- Discharge during peak demand
- Shift flexible loads to solar production windows
- Reduce grid import during high carbon intensity periods
- Reserve battery for outage risk
- Coordinate irrigation energy demand with NeoAgro

## Battery Optimization Objectives

- Maximize renewable self-consumption
- Reduce grid import
- Reduce peak demand
- Reduce carbon
- Protect battery health
- Maintain emergency reserve
- Support critical loads

## SolarHub Example

A heatwave is forecast. NeoAgro predicts higher irrigation demand. NeoGrid predicts lower afternoon solar production and recommends charging batteries during the morning solar peak, then scheduling pumping during the lowest-carbon energy window.

Expected impact:

- Lower grid import
- Lower carbon emissions
- Better water-energy coordination
- Improved resilience
