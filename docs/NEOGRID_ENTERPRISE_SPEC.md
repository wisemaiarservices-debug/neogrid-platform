# NeoGrid Enterprise Specification

## Public Name

NOVA Energy

## Internal Name

NeoGrid

## Parent Platform

NOVA OS — AI Operating System for Sustainable Infrastructure.

## Mission

Help renewable energy operators monitor, forecast, optimize, and report energy performance using AI decision intelligence.

## Problem

Renewable energy systems are fragmented. Operators often use separate systems for solar production, battery telemetry, consumption, grid interaction, weather, carbon reporting, maintenance, and asset management.

This causes poor forecasting, delayed decisions, avoidable grid dependency, and missed carbon reduction.

## Solution

NeoGrid unifies energy data into one NOVA OS module that provides:

- Renewable energy monitoring
- Battery analytics
- Demand forecasting
- Grid optimization
- Carbon analytics
- Digital Twin simulation
- AI recommendations

## Core Modules

- Overview
- Sites
- Energy Assets
- Solar Production

- Grid Operations
- Forecasts

- Carbon

- Reports
