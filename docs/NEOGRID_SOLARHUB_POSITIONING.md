# NeoGrid SolarHub Positioning

## Title

NOVA Energy — Renewable Energy Intelligence powered by NeoGrid

## SolarHub Fit

NeoGrid supports:

- Renewable energy optimization
- Smart control
- Digital Twin for renewable infrastructure
- Predictive analytics
- Carbon analytics
- Microgrid optimization
- AI decision support
- Battery optimization

## Pitch Angle

Renewable energy projects are not only generation assets. They are operational systems requiring forecasting, storage optimization, carbon analytics, predictive maintenance, and intelligent decision support.

NOVA Energy connects energy data, weather data, battery telemetry, grid flow, Digital Twins, and AI recommendations into one decision intelligence module.

## Strongest Demo Story

A heatwave is forecast. Solar production is expected to drop in the afternoon while irrigation energy demand rises.

NeoGrid recommends charging the battery during the morning solar peak and scheduling pump operation during the best energy window.

NeoAgro adjusts irrigation timing. NOVA OS shows expected water savings, grid import reduction, battery impact, and carbon reduction.
